# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Saudi Arabian online shoppers discovering a premium electronics product (earbuds / wireless audio) on a Salla store. They arrive from social media or a direct product link, browse on mobile first, and decide to buy within a single scroll session. They expect a premium, Apple-level feel — not a cluttered market stall.

## Product Purpose

A full-page product landing bundle for Salla merchants selling premium hardware. It exists to convert a cold visitor into a buyer in one scroll: showcase the product's design and features, build trust through specs and reviews, then close with an offer. Success = add to cart.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, premium. The voice is quiet and sure of itself — it doesn't need to shout. Like Apple's product pages: every word earns its space, every section has one job.

## Anti-references

- Generic AliExpress-style clutter: badge overload, discount-first, low-trust aesthetics.
- SaaS startup templates: cream backgrounds, all-caps eyebrows on every section, identical card grids.
- Gimmicky "futuristic": neon gradients, glassmorphism as decoration, particle effects.
- Flat corporate: gray, safe, zero personality — looks like a slide deck.

## Design Principles

1. **One job per section.** Each section answers exactly one question the shopper has. No multi-purpose catch-alls.
2. **Show, don't decorate.** Imagery and motion serve the product, not the page. If a visual element doesn't make the product more desirable, remove it.
3. **Restraint reads as confidence.** Fewer elements, more weight. An empty line is not wasted space.
4. **Arabic-first.** RTL layout, right-to-left reading rhythm, and type choices that serve Arabic script without compromise.
5. **Motion is honest.** Animations reveal what's already there; they never hide content until triggered.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Body text at ≥4.5:1 contrast, large/bold text at ≥3:1. Semantic HTML throughout. Keyboard navigable. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected on all animations — crossfade or instant as fallback. Arabic as primary language; `dir="rtl"` enforced at the component level.
